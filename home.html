College of Engineering Cherthala


DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER SCIENCE

The mission of the Computer Science and Engineering Department of CE Cherthala is to generate high
caliber computer engineers to meet the global demands. The Computer science and Engineering Department
of CEC offers premier professional training with an eye on the modern development in Computer Science
offering 4-year B.Tech degree courses in Computer Science and Engineering, Artificial Intelligence and
Data Science.


DEPARTMENT OF ELECTRONICS

The Electronics Engineering Department of CE Cherthala offers premier professional training with an
eye on the modern development in Electronics Engineering. It offers 4-year B.Tech. degree in Electronics and
Communication Engineering. The curriculum is innovatively designed for giving the students a solid base in core
Electronics. The students acquire a deep knowledge in Electronic Circuits, Micro Processors, networks, and Systems.
